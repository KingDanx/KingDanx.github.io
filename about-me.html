About Me

My name is Dan Prudhomme, Jr and I am 32 years old. I am a single full time father to my 5 year old daughter. We live on the Mississppi Gulf Coast and I commute to New Orleans where I work as an Electronics Technician.
I served over six years in the United States Coast Guard. In 2011, I attended the Electronics Technician "A" School in Petaluma, CA. I have been working in the electronics field since leaving the Coast Guard. In 2018, I took my first real dive into the realm of software development at the University of New Orleans enrolling in a Software Development class. Since then I have had a passion for learning more about the world of software development, and I am continuing my journey to become a developer through devCodeCamp.
The majority of my free time is spent with my daughter. Every weekend we have a day dedicated to do whatever she wants to do within reason. Time that I have to myself is often spent 3D modeling, 3D Printing, and managing a micro business from my home office. I also enjoy playing PC games with friends online.
I have a passion for learning and consuming non-fiction content. I love learning about Astrophysics, Cosmology, Anthropology, and Computer Science because I believe the combination of these fields of study will shape the future for the human race far into the future (that is, if we don't kill ourselves).
